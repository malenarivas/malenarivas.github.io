Fallout 4: Capital Wasteland is a large scale fan-made mod for Fallout 4 that aims to recreate the setting of Fallout 3, focusing on the Capital Wasteland— the devastated region surrounding Washington, D.C. The mod reimagines the familiar world of Fallout 3, allowing players to explore the same locations and encounter similar factions, enemies, and quests, but with the enhanced visuals and mechanics of Fallout 4. It essentially combines the narrative and environment of Fallout 3 with the gameplay improvements and engine of Fallout 4.

I worked on Fallout 4: Capital Wasteland for over a year building over 600 exterior/interior world space cells and contributing to the respective repositories weekly earning the title of Senior Level Designer.

Counter-Strike: Classic Offensive is a fan-made mod for Counter-Strike: Global Offensive that aims to recreate the gameplay and atmosphere of the earlier Counter-Strike titles, particularly Counter-Strike 1.6 and Counter-Strike: Source. It brings back classic maps, weapons, and game modes while retaining the updated engine and mechanics of CS. The mod is designed for players who prefer the older, more traditional Counter-Strike experience with the modern improvements in graphics and performance from CS.

I did all the design, asset, & texture creation on de_nuke and shortly on de_dust 2.

A CS community map is a custom-designed map created by players or independent developers, often featuring unique layouts, themes, and gameplay elements not found in official game maps. These maps are typically shared on platforms like the Steam Workshop, where players can download, play, and provide feedback. Community maps can vary widely in style, from competitive bomb sites (like those in official maps) to creative, fun, or experimental modes, offering fresh experiences for players seeking something different from the standard game modes.

Steelhead was a completely solo project from the layout to the design it was all created by me and was initially released for CS:GO.
