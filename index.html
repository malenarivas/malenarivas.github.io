Aug 14, 2022

This is a Calendar documenting every day of my life. Hopefully. I need to be consistent for this to end up being worth it. The days are sectioned & color-coded by, Great, Good, Mid, Bad, & Horrible. This way I can keep track of my mental health and my days in general. This will be public on my website for the sake of responsibility and accountability. I want this to become a routine since it's very important for me to not only keep track of my life but to keep track of my OCD, Paranoia, Depression, & my general forgetfulness. Im sure I will change things in the future but for now this is the general idea. I hope I've been cohesive.
